Scaling for Growth: Immediate Storage Requirements

VdoCipher Storage Capacity Analysis

Current Capacity Exhausted

Our VdoCipher plan has a strict 100 GB limit. Currently, 85 GB (85%) is already consumed by existing TIH course videos.

Storage Status 85 / 100 GB

📦 The Transcoding Multiplier

We have 2 new courses ready to publish (~100-150 GB compressed). However, VdoCipher automatically generates multiple device-specific copies, inflating the server storage footprint to 200-250 GB per course.

⚡
Action Required: Upgrade 100 GB → 1 TB

To launch the two pending courses and maintain a buffer for future revenue-generating programs, an immediate upgrade to a minimum 1 TB storage capacity is strictly required.
